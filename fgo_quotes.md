# 20 Memorable Fate/Grand Order Quotes

*Filtered from over 100 quotes across various Fate/Grand Order story arcs, events, and Servant dialogue.*

---

### **Epic & Grand Speeches**

1. **The First Hassan (King Hassan) - Babylonia Singularity**
   > "From the mountain abyss, I come, and only death do I bring. I am the Old Man of the Mountain, the First Hassan-i Sabbah. The bell of evening tolls thy name. Touch not the sky, for by heaven's will I shall strip thee of thy wings!"

2. **Dr. Romani Archaman - Final Singularity (Solomon)**
   > "Of course nothing is eternal, and pain awaits us all in the end, but that doesn't make life a story of despair. Not at all. It's a fight against death and separation in what little time we are given."

3. **Arash - Camelot Singularity**
   > "O' Lord of Brightness who bestows all of his power, sanctity, and wisdom to me. See clearly my heart, my thoughts, and my skill. Now, O' being who created the moon and stars... Behold my deeds, my death, my spenta armaiti which I must carry out. —STELLA!"

4. **Gilgamesh (Caster) - Babylonia Singularity**
   > "Bemoaning the obligations that you chose to uphold dutifully is evil, and to praise that pain is an insult only to yourself! What should be praised is the triumphs you achieved!"

5. **Arthur Pendragon (Prototype) - Noble Phantasm**
   > "Seal Thirteen... decision start! ...This is a fight to save the world! Excalibur!!"

### **Emotional & Philosophical**

6. **Patxi - Lostbelt 1: Permafrost Empire Anastasia**
   > "I'll never forgive you, for as long as I live. You know why? Because thanks to you, I now know there's another, happier world out there. So stand up, dammit. Stand up and fight for it... Don't you dare lose to a world like this, a world whose only virtue is strength!"

7. **Oberon Vortigern - Lostbelt 6: Avalon le Fae**
   > "There's still meaning to be found even in the most hackneyed of kingdoms, or the most unsung side characters. All those stories you people from reality just forgot the moment you finished the last page? They had every bit as much right to remain afterward as anything else."

8. **Jeanne d'Arc (Ruler) - Orleans Singularity**
   > "I always strive to be good. I am not stupid, I am not naive. Yes I have been betrayed, but I will not give up being good."

9. **Dr. Romani Archaman - Final Singularity**
   > "We don't live in order to create meaning. We live so meaning can be found from our life."

10. **Miyamoto Musashi - Shimousa Pseudo-Singularity**
